OmniCard® Wallet Features

Eroute is an authorized Payment and Settlement System Operator under the Payment and Settlement Act 2007. It issues semi closed Pre – paid instruments under its registered brand name OmniCard® . (“OmniCard Wallet and Card”) to Holders/Users on which they can load and transfer funds, to make purchases of goods or services.

As per regulatory Guidelines , OmniCard® wallets & cards are issued either on obtaining of minimum details of the Wallet and /or card holder ( “ PPIs with minimum details) which shall include mobile number verified with One Time Pin and self-declaration of name and unique identification number of any of the “ officially valid document” defined under PML Rules,2005 or after completing the KYC of the wallet and /or card holder. . These shall only be issued after completing KYC of the PPI Holder or OmniCard® Wallet and /or Card with loading from a bank account and /or credit card after obtaining minimum details of the User. The minimum details shall necessarily include a mobile number verified with One Time Pin (OTP) and a self-declaration of name and unique identity / identification number of any ‘mandatory document’ or ‘officially valid document’ (OVD) listed in the Master Direction on KYC, as amended from time to time.

OmniCard® Wallet will be valid for an indefinite period while the reloadable card and non-reloadable card will have the expiry of 5 years and 12 months from the date of issuance respectively (as mentioned on the card), subject regulatory guidelines issued from time to time; any changes to the same will be communicated by updating these terms and conditions
